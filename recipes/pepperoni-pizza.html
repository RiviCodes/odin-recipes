Pepperoni pizza

A delicious classic!

The pepperoni pizza is the favorite of most.

Enjoy it even more adding extra cheese!

Ingredients:

For the sauce

  • 1/2 (12 ounce) of tomato paste can
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano (crushed)
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil (crushed)
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper

For the crust

  • 3 ¼ cups all-purpose flour, or more as needed
  • 2 (.25 ounce) envelopes pizza prust
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 1 ⅓ cups very warm water (120 degrees F to 130 degrees F)
  • ⅓ cup oil

Toppings

  • 1 (6 ounce) package of pepperoni
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese, or more to taste

Steps:

  1. For sauce: Combine all sauce ingredients with 1/2 cup water in a medium bowl; set aside for flavors to develop while making crust.
  2. Freeze remaining paste .
  3. For crust: Combine 2 cups of flour with the dry yeast, sugar and salt. Add the water and oil and mix until well blended (about 1 minute).
  4. Gradually add enough remaining flour slowly, until a soft, sticky dough ball is formed.
  5. Knead for about 4 minutes, on a floured surface, until dough is smooth and elastic.
  6. Add more flour, if needed.
  7. Divide dough in half.
  8. Pat each half (with floured hands) into a 12-inch greased pizza pan OR roll dough to fit pans.
  9. For pizzas: Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Top crusts with sauce, pepperoni and cheese.
  10. Bake for 18 to 20 minutes until crusts are browned and cheese is bubbly.
  11. For best results, rotate pizza pans between top and bottom oven racks halfway through baking.
